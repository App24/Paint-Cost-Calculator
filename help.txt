Press Start to start the program
Then press the Get Area button and start entering the height and length of the walls
When finished, select the paint type to paint the walls
The Luxury paint type costs £1.75 per square meter
The Standard paint type costs £1 per square meter
The Economy paint type costs £0.45 per square meter
Choose whether you want undercoat or not
Undercoat costs £0.50 per square meter
Press the finish button to get the price you will have to pay, 
including the price with the undercoat and the price without the undercoat and 
the price of the undercoat
